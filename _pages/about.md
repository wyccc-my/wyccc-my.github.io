I am Yuechao Wang, a PhD student in Mechanical Science and Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

My research focuses on fluid mechanics, including turbulence, Faraday instability, and experimental flow measurements using PIV techniques. I am particularly interested in the interaction between flow structures and non-Newtonian fluid behavior.

I am currently working on:
- Faraday wave experiments in non-Newtonian fluids
- Turbulence modulation by polymers
- Analysis of velocity fields using modal decomposition (POD, DMD, SPOD)

For more details, please see my publications and research pages.
